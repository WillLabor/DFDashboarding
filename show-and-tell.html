Background

What is Delivered Fresh & how does it work?

🥕 The Business

  • Local food hub connecting 50+ farms & producers in Northeast Pennsylvania to homes, businesses and institutions
  • Founded by David & Marla of WindStone Landing Farms — started in 2013 out of frustration with local farm markets, purchased by Will & Jen in January 2026
  • Multiple customer tiers: Retail, Wholesale and Producer
  • Orders placed online Thursday & Friday delivery — pastured meats, organic produce, dairy, baked goods & more

🔗 Why LFM?

  • Local Food Marketplace (LFM) is the platform that runs our storefront, orders, routing and payments
  • Every transaction flows through LFM — making it the single source of truth for our data
  • LFM exposes a full REST API, which means we can pull all our data programmatically
  • Goal: stop relying only on LFM's built-in reports — build our own visibility
The Problem

The questions I kept asking
and couldn't easily answer

"What is our real Average Order Value — and is it going up or down over time?"

"Are we actually winning new customers, or just re-selling to the same people?"

"We ran a promotion — did it actually move the needle, or would sales have been the same anyway?"

"When I try something new, how do I know within a few weeks if it's working for real?"

These aren't hard questions in principle. The data exists. The problem was having no fast, repeatable way to surface the answers.

The Solution

What we built

A live Python dashboard connected directly to LFM — five views, no spreadsheets, no manual exports.

📊 Order Value Analysis
AOV, revenue and order counts by week, month or year. Filter by customer type and spot trends instantly.
👥 Customer Segments
8 RFM lifecycle buckets: Champions, Loyal, At-Risk, New, Lost and more. Know exactly who needs attention today.
💰 Customer Lifetime Value
Projected CLV per customer with 3–36 month windows. High / Medium / Low tiers reveal where the real revenue lives.
🤖 ML Churn & Upgrade Scoring
Random Forest models score every customer 0–100 for churn risk and upgrade potential. Top win-back and upsell lists generated automatically.
📈 Product & Producer Trends
Top categories and producers by order volume over time. Understand what's driving — and dragging — the basket.
Connecting the dots

How the dashboard answers
those exact questions

✅ AOV & trends

Order Value Analysis view — AOV by week/month/year, by customer type. See a dip the same week you ran a discount? Now you can prove it.

✅ New customer acquisition

Customer Segments view — the "New" segment shows first-time buyers in the last 90 days. Track the number across periods to see if growth is real.

✅ Did the promo work?

Order Value Analysis — filter to the promo week/month, compare AOV and order count to the same week prior year or prior period. Evidence, not intuition.

✅ Is this new thing working?

Customer LTV + ML scoring — run scoring before and after. Are churn-risk scores improving? Is upgrader pool growing? Measurable signals in weeks, not quarters.
